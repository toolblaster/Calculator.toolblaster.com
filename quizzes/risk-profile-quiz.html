What's Your Investor Profile?

Understanding your risk tolerance is key to smart investing. Answer 5 quick questions to find your profile!

1. When investing, which statement best describes your primary goal?

2. How would you react if your investment portfolio dropped 20% in a short period?

3. What is your investment time horizon for major goals (like retirement)?

4. How familiar are you with investment products like stocks and mutual funds?

5. Which scenario would make you more uncomfortable?
